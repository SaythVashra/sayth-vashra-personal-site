About me

I'm a composer and guitarist (well, sort of...) from Varadero, Cuba, interested in music and videogames. I've made arrangements of music from Apex Legends, Underhero, Celeste, and many other titles. My biggest inspirations are the Final Fantasy Series OST (especially the pieces composed by Nobuo Uematsu) and Pat Metheny, maybe you can find a piece of both in my music. My biggest dream is to compose an entire OST for a videogame some day.

Well, take a look, and I hope you like my content!!
